Help & Support

If you can't find an answer below, contact our team — details at the bottom.

Account

How do I create an account?

Open the GospelVox app and sign in with Google (Android & iOS) or Apple (iOS). Your account is created the first time you sign in.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app and go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. See our Account Deletion page for the full process and what happens to your data.

I can't sign in.

Check your internet connection, then try signing out of Google or Apple on the device and signing back in. If the problem continues, email support@gospelvox.com with a screenshot.

Coins & Payments

How do Coins work?

Coins are the in-app credit used to pay for Sessions and Bible Sessions with Speakers. You buy a Coin pack from the Wallet screen using Razorpay (cards, UPI, net banking, wallets). Coins are credited to your Wallet immediately on successful payment.

I was charged but didn't receive Coins.

Wait up to 24 hours — sometimes the payment confirmation is delayed. If Coins still haven't arrived, email support@gospelvox.com with the Razorpay Payment ID (visible in the payment confirmation email) and we'll credit the Coins or refund the amount within 5 business days. See the Refund Policy.

Can I get a refund on Coins I haven't used?

Coins are credited immediately and are non-refundable as a rule. Refunds are issued for failed payments, failed Sessions, or payment errors — full details on the Refund Policy page.

In which countries can I purchase Coins?

Coin purchases are currently supported for users transacting from India. Support for additional countries will be enabled as we add new payment rails.

Sessions

How do I start a Session with a Speaker?

Open the home tab, choose a Speaker, and tap Chat or Voice. Coins are billed as the Session runs. Make sure you have notifications and microphone permission turned on for voice Sessions.

The Speaker didn't respond / the Session dropped.

If a Session never connected or dropped because of a Platform fault, Coins are refunded to your Wallet automatically within 24–72 hours. If you don't see the refund, tap Report an issue on the Session summary screen.

Are Sessions recorded?

Voice Sessions are not recorded. Chat Sessions are stored so both parties can refer back to the conversation. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

Speakers (Priests)

How do I become a Speaker?

Open the app and choose the Speaker / Priest role at sign-up. Complete the 3-step registration (personal details, ministry information, ID and ordination certificate), pay the one-time activation fee, and wait for approval.

When will I be paid?

Earnings appear in your Wallet after every completed Session. You can request a withdrawal once you reach the minimum threshold; settlement is typically 5–7 business days from request, subject to KYC and anti-fraud checks. See the Terms Section 8 and the Refund Policy Section 7.

Why was my application rejected?

We may reject applications where ID or ordination documents cannot be verified, where details appear inconsistent, or where the role isn't a fit. You'll see a reason in the in-app rejection screen and can re-apply with corrected information.

Privacy & Safety

How do I report a Speaker or User?

Tap the report icon on the Speaker's profile or on a chat / Session summary. Reports are reviewed and acted on within 24 hours where the content is unlawful or grossly objectionable, in line with Apple's App Store Guideline 1.2 and Play Store policy.

How do I block someone?

Open the profile and tap Block. Blocked Speakers and Users cannot start new Sessions or send messages to you. Manage your block list under Settings → Blocked.

Is GospelVox a substitute for therapy or medical care?

No. Speakers provide spiritual / pastoral guidance only. If you're experiencing a medical emergency, suicidal thoughts, or are in immediate danger, please contact your local emergency services. In India: 112 (emergency), 9152987821 (iCall mental-health helpline), 1860-2662-345 (Vandrevala Foundation).
