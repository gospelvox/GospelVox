# Refund Policy — GospelVox

**Effective Date:** 2 June 2026
**Last Updated:** 2 June 2026

This Refund Policy describes when and how refunds are issued for
purchases made on the GospelVox mobile application and related
services (the "**Platform**"), operated by **Codedu Software
Technologies**.

This Refund Policy is part of, and incorporated into, the
[Terms & Conditions](./TERMS_AND_CONDITIONS.md). Defined terms used
here have the meaning given in the Terms & Conditions.

---

## 2. What you can buy on the Platform

- **Coin packs** — virtual credit used to start chat or voice
  Sessions with Speakers and to access Bible Sessions or other paid
  features.
- **Welcome offer** — a one-time, discounted Coin pack offered to
  first-time buyers.
- **Speaker activation fee** — a one-time fee paid by Speakers to
  start receiving paid Sessions on the Platform.

All purchases are processed by **Razorpay Software Private Limited**
("Razorpay") in INR. Prices shown in the app are inclusive of GST
where applicable.

## 3. Coin packs — refund rules

3.1 **Default rule.** Coins are credited to the User's Wallet
immediately on successful payment. Because Coins are usable from the
moment they are credited, **Coin purchases are non-refundable** once
credited.

3.2 **Payment debited but Coins not credited.** If the bank or
payment instrument is debited and Coins have not appeared in the
Wallet within **24 hours**, contact us with the Razorpay Payment ID
and we will, within **5 business days**:

- credit the Coins purchased, **or**
- if crediting is not possible, refund the full INR amount to the
  original payment source.

3.3 **Duplicate charges.** If you have been charged more than once
for the same Coin order, write to us with all Payment IDs. We will
verify and refund the duplicate amount(s) to the original payment
source within **5–7 business days** of verification.

3.4 **Unauthorised charges.** If you believe a Coin purchase was made
without your authorisation, contact us immediately and consider
contacting your card issuer or bank. We will investigate and, if the
charge is found to be unauthorised, reverse the Coin credit and
refund the amount.

## 4. Sessions — refund and re-credit rules

4.1 **Session failed to connect.** If a Session fails to connect
because of a Platform fault (e.g., our servers, the Agora voice
infrastructure, or a billing system error), any Coins billed for
that Session are **automatically credited back to the User's Wallet**,
typically within 24–72 hours.

4.2 **Session ended prematurely due to Platform fault.** If a Session
ends prematurely due to a Platform fault, the User receives a Coin
credit prorated to the portion of the Session not delivered.

4.3 **Speaker no-show.** If the Speaker accepts a request and does
not arrive, or does not respond within the timeout window shown in
the app, the Coins billed for that Session are credited back to the
User's Wallet.

4.4 **Network / device issues on your side.** Sessions interrupted by
the User's or Speaker's own device, mic, network, or notification
settings are **not refundable**. Test your microphone, notifications,
and connectivity before starting a paid Session.

4.5 **Dissatisfaction.** Coins spent on a Session that connected and
ran for its intended duration are **not refundable** solely on the
ground of dissatisfaction with the Speaker's guidance. If you believe
a Speaker materially breached the Terms & Conditions (for example,
abusive behaviour or solicitation outside the Platform), report the
Session from the post-Session screen — we will investigate and, where
appropriate, issue a goodwill Coin credit and take action against
the Speaker.

4.6 **Reporting a Session issue.** Use the in-app "Report" option on
the Session summary screen, or email **support@gospelvox.com**
with the Session ID, date/time, and a brief description, within
**7 days** of the Session.

## 5. Welcome offer and promotional packs

5.1 The welcome offer and other promotional Coin packs are offered at
a discounted price and are **limited to one redemption per User**
unless stated otherwise.

5.2 If the underlying payment fails or is reversed, the promotional
Coins associated with that purchase will be reversed from the
Wallet.

5.3 Promotional Coins themselves carry no separate cash value and are
not redeemable for cash. Where a promotional offer is withdrawn or
modified, Coins already credited from past redemptions remain valid.

## 6. Speaker activation fee

6.1 The Speaker activation fee is a **one-time non-refundable fee**
that grants the Speaker access to receive paid Sessions on the
Platform. The fee is consumed at the time of activation and is not
refundable on account closure, deactivation, suspension, or any other
ground, except where required by applicable law.

6.2 **Payment debited but activation not granted.** If the fee is
debited and the Speaker account is not activated within 24 hours,
contact us with the Razorpay Payment ID and we will, within
**5 business days**, either activate the account or refund the fee
to the original payment source.

## 7. Speaker earnings — withdrawals and reversals

7.1 Speaker earnings are not "refunds". Earnings are remitted to the
Speaker's verified bank account or UPI ID through the in-app
withdrawal flow, subject to Section 8 of the Terms & Conditions.

7.2 We may hold, deduct from, or reverse Speaker earnings — and pull
back the corresponding amount — where the underlying Session was
fraudulent, was subject to a User chargeback, or was in breach of
the Terms & Conditions.

## 8. Chargebacks

8.1 If you initiate a chargeback through your card issuer or bank
**without first contacting our support team**, we treat this as a
material breach of the Terms & Conditions. We may, while the
chargeback is contested:

- suspend your account;
- reverse Coin credits associated with the disputed transaction;
- pursue recovery of fees and costs associated with the chargeback.

8.2 We will share supporting evidence with the card network or bank
to contest chargebacks we believe to be invalid.

## 9. How long refunds take

| Stage | Typical timeline |
|---|---|
| Acknowledgement of refund request | **48 hours** |
| Verification by GospelVox | **5 business days** |
| Razorpay refund initiation | Immediate on approval |
| Credit to your card / UPI / bank | **5–7 business days** from Razorpay's initiation (depends on issuer) |

Delays beyond these timelines, where caused by the payment network,
issuer, or bank, are outside our control. We will share the Razorpay
Refund ID so you can follow up with your issuer if needed.

## 10. How to request a refund

To request a refund or report a payment issue:

1. **In-app:** open the relevant Wallet transaction or Session summary
   and tap **"Report an issue"**.
2. **By email:** write to **support@gospelvox.com** with:
   - the registered email or phone number on your account;
   - the Razorpay Payment ID and/or Session ID;
   - a brief description of the issue.
3. **Grievance escalation:** if you are not satisfied with our
   response within 15 calendar days, write to our Grievance Officer
   (see Section 22 of the Terms & Conditions).
